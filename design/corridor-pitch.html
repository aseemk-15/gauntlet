Why this direction

Coverage is the trust; evidence is the proof; the gate is the precision

Clinical partner

"The swarm instills trust that many dimensions were covered." Physicians trust enumerated coverage — the review-of-systems, the surgical checklist. The field names its ten dimensions and shows three examiners each; the seal persists after the motion ends.

Alert fatigue

33–96% of CDS alerts are ignored. Our answer is spatial: the judge gate is where alert fatigue goes to die, on screen — 27 attacks refuted with reasons before anything reached the physician. The gate count is the precision story told in one number.

The hosts' pattern

Abridge's Linked Evidence traces every note sentence to source audio. Survivor streaks that land on the exact chart span and become lettered exhibits are the same trust mechanic, pointed at safety.

What changed from 02: the swarm returns as the left flank of a three-zone corridor — field → gate → document — so an attack's whole life is one continuous motion instead of disconnected panels. The judge becomes a visible membrane with a body count. The run mints a ten-sector coverage seal that both surfaces share: full theater in the mission log, quiet enumeration in the physician view. Palette, serif verdicts, and the lit-paper document carry over from 02 unchanged.
